SelectWise

Project Selection Tool

1. How well does the project align with our overall strategic plan/project roadmap?

A) Perfectly (90% to 100% alignment)
B) Somewhat (70% to 90% alignment)
C) Meh (50% to 70% alignment)
D) Not Very Well (Less than 50% alignment)

2. How critical is the project right now?

A) It's super critical; it's a predecessor to one or more other projects.
B) It's something we need to do, but it can wait.
C) It's not something we had considered, but it could be important at some point.
D) It won't be required at any time.

3. How many additional team members (new hires or contractors) will it require?

A) None
B) 1-2
C) 3-5
D) 6 or more

4. How long will the project realistically take to complete?

A) 0-6 months
B) 7-12 months
C) 1-3 years
D) More than 3 years

5. Where will funding for the project come from?

A) From revenue generated by the project.
B) New funding - a grant or other award.
C) New funding - a loan or other debt service.
D) We have no idea.
